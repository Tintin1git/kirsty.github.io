About Me

I am an ICT and EdTech professional with a strong focus on cybersecurity, digital forensics, and digital literacy. I specialize in ICT support, educational technology integration, digital literacy training, and AV systems deployment across learning institutions, delivering end-to-end support from system setup and troubleshooting to training and long-term sustainability.

My work involves supporting and maintaining IT infrastructure, implementing digital learning solutions, and facilitating hands-on training for both educators and learners. I also contribute to building secure digital environments by applying cybersecurity principles to protect systems, analyse threats, and promote safe technology use.

Beyond the technical space, I am deeply committed to digital empowerment. I actively support initiatives that promote digital literacy, computing education, and responsible technology use, particularly among young people and underserved communities.

By combining technical expertise with a passion for education and inclusion, I aim to bridge the gap between advanced technology and community development, creating secure, resilient systems while empowering individuals to confidently navigate the digital world.
